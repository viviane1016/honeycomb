## How the queen uses your briefing

At plan time, the queen reads your briefing and:

1. Classifies it against tags (security, auth, performance, etc.) to determine what honeycomb S-SDLC rooms are relevant.
2. Calls `palace_recall()` to fetch guidance.
3. Designs the work breakdown into specs.
4. Documents her reasoning in the plan's `## Context` section.

The clearer your briefing, the better the queen's classification and recall.
