**Two build-and-ship gates:** Plan approval (is this the right thing?), PR review (is this acceptable?). **Reflection-stage gate:** Per-action approval for non-git actions (GitHub issues, external notifications). **Debug inline-fix policy:** Governed by queen-file default `confirm-each`; operators may set `fix-then-notify` per-repo. **Colony advisory gates:** scribe-verify and queen-accept are internal, not human gates; operator amendments go in `briefing.md ## Operator amendments`.
