Design produces ADRs (status: Proposed) and design doc updates — not a work breakdown. The design actor browses decisions/, design docs, and BACKLOG to propose correctly-numbered ADRs and avoid contradictions. One session may produce multiple ADRs. The human gate is review of proposed ADRs before they are committed. If implementation should follow, the actor produces a recommended briefing for bees plan — but this is optional.
