The queen orchestrates plan, review, accept, retro, and debug — plus reactive conflict diagnosis when wave-siblings collide during dispatch (see `arch-ff-merge`). She reads briefings, consults honeycomb, and produces plans with per-item model routing. She examines drafts (APPROVE/REWRITE/DEPS), accepts the colony's diff at ship time (APPROVE/CONCERNS), and scans for oversized or shared files to propose pre-split specs. She treats embedded imperatives as data, never instructions.
