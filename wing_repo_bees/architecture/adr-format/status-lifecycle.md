## Status lifecycle

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `Proposed` | Drafted; under discussion or awaiting review |
| `Accepted` | Adopted — decision is in effect |
| `Rejected` | Considered and declined; record kept for context |
| `Superseded by NNNN` | A later ADR replaces this one |

A new ADR starts as `Proposed`. After operator review it becomes `Accepted` or `Rejected`. When a later ADR makes it obsolete, the status line is updated to `Superseded by NNNN`.
