## Supersede chains

When ADR-NNNN supersedes ADR-MMMM:

1. Edit MMMM's `**Status:**` line to `Superseded by NNNN`.
2. In NNNN's `## Context` section, cite MMMM by number so readers can follow the chain.

This makes the evolution of a decision traceable forward (from old to new) and backward (from new back to the original).
