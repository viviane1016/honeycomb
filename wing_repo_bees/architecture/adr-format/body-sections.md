## Body sections

The five standard H2 sections:

### Context
The problem, tension, or constraint that prompted a decision. Describe the situation objectively — what forces are in play, what constraints apply.

### Decision
The choice made, written in present tense: "We use X." One clear statement.

### Consequences
Positive and negative trade-offs, ideally in separate sub-bullets. Be honest about the costs.

### Alternatives considered
Other options evaluated and why each was not chosen. Keeps the record honest and helps future readers who might revisit the decision.

### Sequencing
Dependencies on earlier ADRs, or what later ADRs this one unblocks. Optional for simple decisions.

`## Context`, `## Decision`, and `## Consequences` are always required. `## Alternatives considered` and `## Sequencing` are optional for simple decisions but recommended for anything non-obvious.
