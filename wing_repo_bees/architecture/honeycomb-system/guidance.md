## Guidance

Honeycomb is content, not code. Operators curate it between releases; it evolves at its own cadence. If you find a gap (methodology not in honeycomb), you can emit a petition at retro or debug, but don't expect instant answers. Honeycomb is what the queen knows; gaps in honeycomb are gaps in the queen's guidance. The queen-file bridges this: project-specific insights can queue there locally, and approved petitions copy to the queen-file for future plans before global curation happens.
