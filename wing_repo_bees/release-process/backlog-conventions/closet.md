BACKLOG.md uses `##` release-grouping headings (optional `(shipped YYYY-MM-DD)` or `(target: …)` suffix), `###` sub-cluster headings within a release, and `-` bulleted items. Shipped items: `- ~~**title**~~ **Shipped YYYY-MM-DD.**` (period inside or outside title strike, both forms valid). Rationale under struck items is retained verbatim — BACKLOG is a historical record. Deferred items keep rationale and gain a parenthetical deferral note.
