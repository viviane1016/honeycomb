Conventional commits format: `<type>(<scope>): <description>` + body explains why. Types: feat, fix, refactor, docs, test, chore, build, ci, perf, style, revert. First line ≤72 chars, imperative mood. Body wraps at ~72 chars. Footer: `Refs #N`, `Closes #N`, `BREAKING CHANGE:`. Example: `feat(auth): add OAuth login flow` + body explaining why OAuth was chosen over SAML.
